About The Common Ground

The Common Ground is a community-centered media initiative I created to bring together the voices, stories, and work of minority communities across New York City. Throughout many neighborhoods, individuals and organizations are doing meaningful work, yet there are few spaces that truly connect these efforts or highlight the people behind them. The Common Ground was created to help fill that gap through storytelling, journalism, interviews, and collaborative media projects. The platform focuses on the lived experiences of immigrants, youth, workers, families, and historically underrepresented communities. More than just a publication, it aims to serve as a shared space for dialogue, creativity, and community connection across different generations and backgrounds. What began as an idea in November 2025 gradually developed into an active project, officially launching in March 2026. In April 2026, The Common Ground conducted its first major interview featuring New York State Assembly candidate Shamsul Haque.
